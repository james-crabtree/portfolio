Neuroplasicity and Growth Mindset.

According to the definition on Google when you look up neuroplasticity, it is, the ability of the brain to form and reorganize synaptic connections, especially in response to learning or experience or following injury. Understanding neuroplasticity helps benefit people in their ability to learn new things, enhance existing cognitive abilities and learnings, and can also strengthen cognitive areas where function has been lost or has declined over time. In some other cases, neuroplasticity can help stroke and traumatic brain injury patients recover and improve their cognitive function.

Emotional Intelligence.

When you look up emotional intelligence on Google it will come up with this definition - “the capacity to be aware of, control, and express one's emotions, and to handle interpersonal relationships judiciously and empathetically”. But what does that actually mean?
To put it into more simple and more understandable terms, Emotional intelligence is the ability to notice, understand and manage your emotions and the emotions of others around you, like your family, friends, colleagues. There are five key elements to EI: self-awareness, self-regulation, motivation, empathy, and social skills.

HTML and CSS concepts.

From an outsiders perspective, classes and id’s may look the same, they essentially both do the same thing - they’re both used to edit elements with CSS, but taking a deeper closer look into the two, you’ll see that their uses are different to each other. To put it into simple terms, you should use a class when you plan to use it on multiple elements (like on a h1, p, h3, div) while you should use an id if you are only trying to edit a specific single element.
